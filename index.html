About the Role

We're seeking our first illustrator to help complete artwork for The Great American Road Trip. This is a foundational role where you'll shape the visual identity of our debut game. We need someone who can create vibrant, family-friendly illustrations that bring the game to life.

What You'll Create

  • City and location cards featuring U.S. destinations
  • Event and action cards with engaging scenarios
  • Game board elements and visual components
  • Box art and marketing materials

What We're Looking For

  • Strong illustration and graphic design portfolio
  • Proficiency in Adobe Creative Suite or similar tools
  • Ability to create consistent visual style across multiple assets
  • Passion for games and understanding of what makes game art effective

Compensation

  • $28-30 per hour (W-2 employee)
  • Bonus payment when game ships
  • Performance, royalty-based incentives
  • Flexible remote work schedule
  • Portfolio-building opportunity

How to Apply

Send the following to careers@obeegames.com:

  • Your resume or CV
  • Link to your portfolio (Behance, ArtStation, personal site, etc.)
  • Brief note about your interest in board game illustration
  • Your favorite board game and why you love it

Applications reviewed immediately. We're excited to hear from you!
